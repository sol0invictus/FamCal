# FamCal Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-06-27_

> **Note:** This is a starting-point template, not legal advice. Have it reviewed by a
> qualified professional before publishing a paid app, and host it at a public URL to link
> from the Google Play listing and from inside the app.

FamCal ("we", "the app") is a shared family calendar. This policy explains what we collect,
why, and your choices.

## Information we collect
- **Account information:** your name and email address, provided when you sign up (via email
  or Google sign-in).
- **Calendar content:** events you create — titles, notes, locations, dates/times, reminders,
  and recurrence — and the families and members you belong to.
- **Device push token:** an identifier used to send you notifications about changes your
  family members make.
- **Diagnostics:** crash reports and basic diagnostic data (via Firebase Crashlytics) to help
  us fix problems.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## How we use it
- To provide the shared calendar and sync it between members of your family.
- To send reminders and notifications you have enabled.
- To maintain, secure, and improve the app.

## Who can see your data
- **Members of a family** can see that family's events and member names.
- **Service providers:** we use Google Firebase (Authentication, Cloud Firestore, Cloud
  Messaging, Crashlytics) to operate the app; data is stored on Google Cloud infrastructure
  subject to Google's terms.

## Device permissions
- **Notifications:** to deliver reminders and update alerts.
- **Calendar (optional):** only if you enable "Sync to this phone's calendar," the app writes
  your family's events into a calendar account you choose on your device. You can turn this off
  any time in Settings, which removes those mirrored events.

## Optional calendar subscription
If you subscribe to your family's calendar feed from Google Calendar or Outlook, anyone with
that private feed URL can view the family's events. Treat the URL as a secret.

## Data retention and deletion
Your data is kept while your account exists. You can **delete your account and associated data
at any time** in the app: **Settings → Delete account**. This removes your profile and your
membership in all families. For help, contact us at the address below.

## Children
FamCal is intended for general audiences and is not directed to children under 13. Do not
create an account for a child under the age required by your local law.

## Changes
We may update this policy; material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
